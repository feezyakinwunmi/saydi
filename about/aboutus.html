OUR HISTORY

A non-governmental organization in Africa,Nigeria with the mandate to promote equitable quality education; campaign againts gender disparity and civic engagement apathy; and advocacy for the achievevment of sustainable Development Goals(SDGs) in immediate communities and beyond

SAYDI was launched as a student organization in 2020 at the university of Ilorin,Nigeria and was officially registered with the corporate affairs commission and a non-profit organization in the year 2021. The organization has since grown to be a worthy NGO beign run by a population of youth volunteers.

SAYDI supports marginalized and vulnarable communities with resources that amplifies their voice while also providing them with information that foster social and economic progress.This we do without demand yet transparent and unimpaired in our engagements.

To enhance effective education, democratic governance and social justice. SAYDI creates that close feedback floop between citizen and the government.With global expertise and reach, we focus on community outreach, influencing policies, practices, and knowledge mobilization.

OUR VISION

To create and advocate for an ecosystem of opportunities where young people and especially the underserved can fully harness their potentials and optimally unlock it to their advantage ad that of the entire society.

OUR MISSION

Host and engage in activities that encourages Active Participation, Social Inclusion, Equity and Balance in Education, Governance Decision Making Process

OUR OBJECTIVE

Balance and Inclusiveness in Education, Self-discovery, and positive charge in the society.

Promote gender equity as a principle of human rigth and a development goal

Advocate gender rebalancing in resources distribution, legal right, and politial participation.

Contribute positively to the achievement of good governance, tranparency, accountability, and people-friendly legal framework for youth dreams.

To encourage youth participation in politics and decision making.
